## Who We Are

PrintForge Studio is a one-person 3D printing workshop dedicated to producing high-quality prints at an affordable price. Whether you need a detailed miniature for your tabletop campaign, a functional replacement part, or a unique piece of home decor — we've got you covered.

## Our Setup

We run a fleet of FDM printers (Bambu Lab X1 Carbon, Prusa MK4) alongside an MSLA resin printer (Elegoo Saturn 3) for ultra-fine detail work. Every print is inspected before it leaves the shop.

## Materials

- **PLA & PLA+** — our go-to for most display and decorative pieces
- **PETG** — when heat resistance or durability matters
- **ABS / ASA** — for tough, outdoor-suitable parts
- **TPU** — flexible, grip-friendly prints
- **Resin** — for miniatures and high-detail objects

## Lead Times

Most orders are completed within **3–7 business days** depending on complexity and queue depth. Rush orders may be accommodated — just ask.
